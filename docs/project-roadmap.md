# SCHub.vn Project Roadmap

## Positioning

SCHub.vn is a Vietnamese Supply Chain practical knowledge platform combining:
- Content Hub
- Free Checklist / Lead Magnet
- Digital Products
- Tools / Calculators
- Consulting Services

## 90-Day Goal

Within 90 days:
- Public website MVP
- 20 practical articles
- 1 free checklist lead magnet
- 1 mini eBook
- 3 digital templates
- First product sale or consulting lead

## Product Ladder

1. Free articles
2. Free checklist / template
3. Paid digital products
4. Bundles / playbooks
5. Consulting / dashboard / process standardization

## Core Digital Products

- 30 Công Cụ Quản Trị Supply Chain Thực Chiến
- ABC/XYZ Inventory Analysis Template
- 3PL SLA & Cost Evaluation Pack
- Supply Chain KPI Dashboard Starter Pack
- Master Data Audit Checklist
- S&OP Calendar & Meeting Pack
